Food Bud's outstanding team planned and executed excellently the distribution of meals.

By Djlord Sucat, September 22, 2023

We had an event last September 22 with Food Bud Events and Catering Services for my nephew’s 7th Birthday, and they have provided an awesome experience with their creative setup and amazing group of staff.

We actually didn't expect the amount of attendees as it exceeded the number of guests we anticipated, and we're quite anxious in terms of quantity. But to our surprise, Food Bud's outstanding team planned and executed excellently the distribution of meals. In this regard, kudos to the coordinator Sir Loyd, the head waiter Glen, and his fantastic team of waiters (James, Kenken, Luwi, and Lance). These guys deserved a recognition for their exemplary performance and resolute professionalism in achieving the success of our event.

In addition to this, we would also like to extend our utmost gratitude to the host/magician and his assistant (which I heard was his wife) for their seamless work and gratifying performance.

Overall, words aren’t sufficient to express how pleased we are. And we’ll definitely recommend this event and catering services to anyone who’d like to have a fanciful and extraordinary event in the future!
